Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Stack Up Stack! does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All game data is stored locally on your device only.

1. Information We Collect

Stack Up Stack! does not collect any personally identifiable information. The following data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to any server:

  • High score
  • Game statistics (total stacks, best combo, survival time, etc.)
  • Sound and music preferences

2. How Data Is Stored

Game data is stored using Android's local storage (AsyncStorage). This data remains entirely on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

3. Third-Party Services

The current version of Stack Up Stack! does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app requires the following permissions solely for core functionality:

  • INTERNET – Reserved for future optional features
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE – Reserved for future optional features
  • VIBRATE – Used for haptic feedback during gameplay

4. Children's Privacy

Stack Up Stack! is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since no data is collected at all, the app fully complies with COPPA and similar regulations.

5. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data breach related to user information. All game state is stored locally and is under the user's full control.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the new version will be published at this URL. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Your Rights

Since no personal data is collected, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. You can remove all local game data at any time by uninstalling the app.
